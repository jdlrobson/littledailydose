二 èr

Two (Numeral) / Second (sequence) reference: 1.01

二老 (wǒ jiā èr lǎo) Parents Literal. Two old (folks); 二维 (èr wéi) Two-dimensional; 二维码 (èr wéi mǎ) QR Code Literal. Two- dimensional code; 二手 (èr shǒu) Second-hand; 二手货 (èr shǒu huò) Second-hand goods; 二手店 (èr shǒu diàn) Second-hand shop; 二代 (èr dài) Second generation; 二婚 (èr hūn) Second marriage

Personal note:

二 is the trickiest numeral to grasp! Although 二 and 两 (liǎng) both mean “Two”, the latter is widely used instead of the former to indicate the amount (often with Classifiers, reference: 3.08 个).
  • 两个人 (liǎng gè rén) Two people
  • 两片蛋糕 (liǎng piàn dàn gāo) Two slices of cake
  • 两碗面 (liǎng wǎn miàn) Two bowls of noodles
  • 两天 (liǎng tiān) Two days
  • 两个月 (liǎng gè yuè) Two months
  • 两月 (liáng nián) Two years
二 (èr) is used to tell date/ me with the excep on of “Two o’clock”:
  • 二零 (èr líng yī èr nián) Year 2012
  • 二(èr yuè) February
  • 二 (èr yuè shí èr rì) February the twelfth
  • 两 (liǎng diǎn) Two o’clock
  • 二 (shí èr diǎn) Twelve o’clock
  • 两 二 (xià wǔ liáng diǎn shí èr fēn) 2:12pm
  • 二 (zhōng wǔ shí ér diǎn èr shí fēn) 12:20pm
